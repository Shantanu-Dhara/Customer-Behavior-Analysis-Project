# Customer Behavior Analysis Project

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing customer shopping behavior for a retail company to uncover valuable business insights and support data-driven decision-making.

The objective of this project is to understand how customer demographics, discounts, product categories, reviews, shipping preferences, and subscription status influence purchasing behavior and customer loyalty.

The project was completed using Python for data cleaning and transformation, SQL for business analysis, and Power BI for interactive dashboard visualization.

---

# Business Problem Statement

A leading retail company wants to better understand its customers’ shopping behavior in order to improve sales, customer satisfaction, and long-term loyalty.

The company observed changing purchasing patterns across:
- Customer demographics
- Product categories
- Sales behavior
- Subscription preferences
- Discounts and promotions
- Payment methods
- Shipping types

The goal of this analysis is to answer:

> “How can the company leverage consumer shopping data to identify trends, improve customer engagement, and optimize marketing and product strategies?”

# Key Insights

- Subscribed customers contributed significantly higher revenue.
- Discount strategies increased purchase frequency for specific products.
- Loyal customers showed stronger repeat purchase behavior.
- Certain product categories consistently achieved higher review ratings.
- Shipping preferences influenced average purchase amounts.
- Middle-aged customers generated the highest revenue contribution.
